Our Mission

We aim to bring together people who find joy in nature, whether that’s climbing, hiking, or just enjoying a campfire with good company.

History of MEC

Founded in 1999 by a group of backpackers in Colorado, MEC has grown into a global network of nature lovers.

“We started with muddy boots and big dreams.” — Founder, Alex Woods
